Bag-of-words model

The primary technique used for recommendation is the bag-of-words model. Using the reviews, we constructed a dictionary of words that were included, and sorted them by frequency.
After filtering for only those words that help identify wine flavors, we arrived at the following top 15 most commonly used words.
The goal is to have users input description keywords such as the above and have our algorithm identify reviews that contain those keywords.
The first keyword will be weighted higher than the second keyword, the second keyword will be weighted higher than the third keyword, and so forth. The "recommendation" column will contain our level of recommendation: the higher the value, the greater match the wine is with your indicated preferences.
We also have filters for country and variety available for users who have specific preferences with respect to these categories.
